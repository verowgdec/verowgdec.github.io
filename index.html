Welcome to Siloam Pool Ministry located in Kalisizo TC, Kyotera District, uGANDA

Siloam Pool Ministries is a born again Christian Church located in Kyotera District within Kalisizo Town Council. The ministry was established with a God-ordained responsibility of sharing the word of Jesus Christ. Siloam Pool Ministries has an estalished orphanage supporting more than 250 children in the heart of Kalisizo Town Council. To carry on this resposibility we are financially supported by contributions from friends and partners of the ministry. General Events.

Siloam Pool Ministry strives to be a warm and welcoming place for all. To foster a sense of community and belonging, we host a variety of special events throughout the year. These events provide opportunities to connect with fellow members, explore your faith in new ways, and participate in activities that uplift and inspire.

Seasonal Celebrations.

We cherish the major Christian holidays like Easter, Christmas, and Thanksgiving with special services, festive decorations, and potluck meals. These celebrations are a beautiful way to come together and reflect on the significance of the season.

Fellowship Events.

Throughout the year, we organize picnics, barbecues, game nights, and movie nights. These casual gatherings are a fantastic way to relax, have fun, and build lasting friendships with other church members.

Spiritual Growth Initiatives.

We offer special workshops, seminars, and guest speaker sessions on various faith-based topics. These events provide opportunities to deepen your understanding of the Bible, explore different aspects of Christian life, and gain practical tools for spiritual growth.

Community Outreach Activities.

Siloam Pool Ministry is committed to serving those in need. We organize volunteer drives, clothing collections, and fundraising events to support local charities and community initiatives. Participating in these activities allows you to connect with your community and make a positive impact.
