Welcome to VEROWGDEC
"Thousands of children in Uganda become orphans after the death of there parents leaving them in gaps of limited accessibility to quality education, clothing and shelter . Help us to make there lives cheerful."

We are a registered non profit Commmunity based organization 6828 working to bring hope and love to vulnerable children, orphans, women and Girl Child in Uganda-Africa, Kyotera District. VERWOGDEC was founded in 2007 as a group of 6 women and four men. In Kalisizo town where VEROWGDEC is located spans to be one of the places that witnessed the first outbreaks of HIV/AIDS in Uganda before it spread to other parts of Uganda...Read More
