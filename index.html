What we are doing

Giving a helping hand to children, Women & Girl Child

What we do

We empower women, girl child, and the orphaned the necessary foundations of hope, love and care any previleged citizen has access to.

Our Vision


To see women, girl child and the orphaned living an equal life in all spheres of life like any other global citizen.

Our Mission


To impact the lives of women, girlchild and the orphaned through a project based approach.

Our Background

We are a registered non profit, non Governmental, Non racism, Non religious Commmunity based organization 6828. VEROWGDEC was founded in 2007 by Ayesiza Christine with a group of six women and four men with a major focus of : 1. Decampaining Gender Based Violence. 2. Supporting Orphans so that they can escape the trap of emotional torture and trauma due to the lack of parental love and support. 3. Setting up Ddembelyabato Primary School project that will educate orphans at primary levels who cannot afford quality education. 4. Empowering Wowen and Girl Child through various community summits and providing them with life changing skills.
