WHAT WE DO

We provide women, girl child, and orphans basic needs and awareness to bring about long-term sustainable changes in their lives.



OUR VISION

Our vision is to see Women and Girl Child's lives transformed, children including orphans and communities empowered to ultimately change their communities through giving practical solutions

OUR MISSION

To see women and Girl child in Uganda having a firm voice in the practical spheres of life.




About Us

Our History

We are a registered non profit Commmunity based organization 6828 working to bring hope and love to vulnerable children, orphans, women and Girl Child in Uganda-Africa, Kyotera District. We founded VERWOGDEC in 2007 as a group of 6 women and four men. In Kalisizo town where VEROWGDEC is located spans to be one of the places that witnessed the first outbreaks of HIV/AIDS in Uganda before it spread to other areas.
