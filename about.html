About

Welcome to Siloam Pool Ministries

Siloam Pool Ministries is a born again Christian Church located in Kyotera District within Kalisizo Town Council. The ministry was established with a God-ordained responsibility of sharing the word of Jesus Christ.

Siloam Pool Ministries has an estalished orphanage supporting more than 250 children in the heart of Kalisizo Town Council. To carry on this resposibility we are financially supported by contributions from friends and partners of the ministry. Kalisizo Town coucil is among the top 3 areas in the entire Uganda which witnessed the massive outbreak of HIV/AIDS. The ministry was established to provide hope through a Christ-centered approach.

Our Purpose

Siloam Pool Ministry is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to Spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and helping people grow in their faith.

Our Mission

Our Mission Statement: Siloam Pool Ministry is dedicated to promoting Spiritual Growth. We provide opportunities for individuals to deepen their relationship with God through prayer, Bible study, and fellowship.


Our Values

Drawing inspiration from the story of Siloam in John 9:7, where Jesus heals a blind man, Siloam Pool Ministry upholds the following core values to guide our mission and foster a thriving faith community:

Love: We cultivate a welcoming and loving environment, reflecting God's unconditional love for all. We treat each other with compassion, kindness, and respect, fostering a sense of belonging and support.

Faith: We stand firm in the teachings of the Bible and encourage a deepening faith in Jesus Christ. We provide opportunities for spiritual growth through worship services, Bible studies, and small groups.

Healing: Inspired by the healing waters of Siloam, we believe in the transformative power of God's grace. We offer support and resources for those seeking spiritual, emotional, or physical healing.

Service: We are called to serve one another and our community. We encourage acts of compassion, justice, and generosity, following the example of Jesus Christ.

The Challenges

Our current facilities limit our ability to serve the growing needs of our community. We are facing a critical shortage of space, hindering our efforts to expand our programs and reach more individuals.
1. Limited Land: Our current property restricts us from adding essential facilities, such as classrooms for educational programs or a larger communal space for gatherings and events.
2. Insufficient Seating: The lack of adequate church chairs makes it difficult to comfortably accommodate our congregation during services and events.
3. Un-plastered Floor: The un-plastered earthen floor creates an uneven and dusty environment, posing health and safety concerns for our patrons.

The Solution

To address these challenges, Siloam Pool Ministries is embarking on an expansion project. This project encompasses three key components:
1. Land Acquisition: We aim to secure additional land adjacent to our existing property. This will allow us to construct new facilities to better serve the community.
2. Church Chair Procurement: By acquiring a sufficient number of church chairs, we can ensure comfortable seating for our congregation and visitors during services and events.
3. Floor Plastering: Plastering the current floor will create a clean, even, and healthy environment for all who utilize our facilities.

The Impact

By successfully completing this expansion project, Siloam Pool Ministries we will achieve the following:
1. Increased Capacity: The additional land will enable us to construct new facilities, such as classrooms for educational programs and a larger communal space, allowing us to serve a greater number of people in our community.
2. Enhanced Comfort: New church chairs will provide comfortable seating for our congregation, fostering a more welcoming and inclusive atmosphere.
3. Improved Health and Safety: A plastered floor will create a clean and healthy environment, minimizing dust and potential health hazards.

Sustainability

Siloam Pool Ministries is committed to the long-term sustainability of our programs. We have a proven track record of responsible financial management and a dedicated team of volunteers committed to the mission.
