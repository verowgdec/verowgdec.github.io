We Are Siloam Pool Ministry

Siloam Pool Ministries is a born again Christian Church located in Kyotera District within Kalisizo Town Council. The ministry was established with a God-ordained responsibility of sharing the word of Jesus Christ. Siloam Pool Ministries has an estalished orphanage supporting more than 250 children in the heart of Kalisizo Town Council. To carry on this resposibility we are financially supported by contributions from friends and partners of the ministry. Kalisizo Town coucil is among the top 3 areas in the entire Uganda which witnessed the massive outbreak of HIV/AIDS. The ministry was established to provide hope to the people in Kalisizo Town Council.

Our Purpose.

Siloam Pool Ministry is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to Spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and helping people grow in their faith.

Our Mission.

Siloam Pool Ministry draws inspiration from the healing waters of the Pool of Siloam in Jerusalem. Just as those waters brought physical restoration, we strive to offer spiritual, emotional, and practical healing to our community.

Our Mission Statement: Siloam Pool Ministry is dedicated to promoting Spiritual Growth. We provide opportunities for individuals to deepen their relationship with God through prayer, Bible study, and fellowship.

Our Values.

Drawing inspiration from the story of Siloam in John 9:7, where Jesus heals a blind man, Siloam Pool Ministry upholds the following core values to guide our mission and foster a thriving faith community:

Love: We cultivate a welcoming and loving environment, reflecting God's unconditional love for all. We treat each other with compassion, kindness, and respect, fostering a sense of belonging and support.

Faith: We stand firm in the teachings of the Bible and encourage a deepening faith in Jesus Christ. We provide opportunities for spiritual growth through worship services, Bible studies, and small groups.

Healing: Inspired by the healing waters of Siloam, we believe in the transformative power of God's grace. We offer support and resources for those seeking spiritual, emotional, or physical healing.

Service: We are called to serve one another and our community. We encourage acts of compassion, justice, and generosity, following the example of Jesus Christ.

Inclusion: We believe that all people are created equal in the image of God. We welcome individuals of all backgrounds, beliefs, and walks of life to join our faith community.

Stewardship: We are responsible stewards of God's gifts. We practice financial integrity and use our resources wisely to support our ministry and outreach programs.

Excellence: We strive for excellence in all that we do, from our worship services to our community outreach programs. We believe that honoring God means offering our best.
